UX designer / Frontend Developer

Hello! You can call me taro

I am a Developer from Guatemala, currently working for Pixela, a company I co-founded with some of my friends. At Pixela I work beside my team mates designing useful, usable and rich web and mobile applications. I like to work close with the project owners and users, to create amazing experiences and improve people's life. While I was at school I joined some non-profit organizations, and helped them develop useful tools, to take care of their information.

My soul is divided between arts, humanity and science. I decided to become a frontend developer since i think programming is the perfect mix between those three things. Arts are a very important for me since i was a kid, thus I started to learn music and I also spend my time writing cheesy lines on my tumblr.

I like to give some time to causes I care about. I like teaching and learning, and love kids (and their super creative mind) which is why I often teach kids math, arts and music.
